The Gardens by the Bay is a nature park spanning 101 hectares in the Central Region of Singapore, adjacent to the Marina Reservoir. The park consists of three waterfront gardens: Bay South Garden, Bay East Garden and Bay Central Garden.

marina bay sands

I have visited in feb 2022.

Across from Marina Bay, this high-end hotel within a sprawling entertainment complex is 1 km from greenhouses at Gardens by the Bay and 18 km from Singapore Changi Airport. Polished rooms feature Wi-Fi, flat-screens and minibars, plus sitting areas and floor-to-ceilings windows. Upgraded rooms add city or bay views. Suites have living areas, kitchenettes, saunas, pianos and/or butler service. Room service is offered 24/7. Amenities include restaurants, bars, cafes and lounges, as well as event space, a casino and a museum. There's also a spa, a gym and an observation deck, along with a rooftop infinity pool. Breakfast is available.
